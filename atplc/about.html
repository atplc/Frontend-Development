About Us

Introduction

A Technical & Practical Learning Club(ATPLC) is a technical and practical club which aimed at providing practical learning experience and an all-round development to the tech-students. Initiated in 2020 from Motihari College of Engineering, Motihari Campus, through this club we aims at motivating students to learn proper set of skills apart from semester syllabus which is required to make them stand apart from mob.

This club also helps students to sort out problems and confusion they are facing during learning any specific skill.Apart from that it also provide them a platform to show their existing skill & also help them to nurture it by competing themselves with other same skill occupying students.And overall these procedure help them in developing a proper stack of skill which will help them in placement or while working in organization. Apart from all these, this club also helps students to clear doubt related to their semester subjects by organizing discussion and doubt clearing classes. So,overall this club focus at helping a student in their all-round development to become fit for professional world.

Our Vision

ATPLC aims at all round development of students enriching their technical knowledge. We believe that today's world is a highly competitive world. And in this highly competitive world, every organization wants to be the best and hire the best.So, an experience holding candidate are given more preference than the fresher one.And worse thing is that our education system being theoretical one,unable to teach us the skills needed for professional world. So, ATPLC is a platform overcoming this shortcoming.

Through this platform, we are focusing to enhance practical learning and focus on learning those professional skills which will help us to get success in professional world after graduating from the college.And for we are focusing towards learning those skills which help us in qualifying placement interview.

So, our overall vision of this club is to create an interactive learning environment where we together learn and gain different knowledge and skills through practically implementing it so that we had proper experience regarding that work which will helps us to help us during working in a company/organization.

Note-: If you too have such vision for your career, then join with us. We assure that you will learn a lot. For more details, contact us.
